Crop Health

This week’s field focus

For paddy and maize under high humidity, experts advise close scouting for fungal spots on lower leaves. Avoid evening irrigation if night humidity stays above 80%.

  • Scout early morning or late afternoon.
  • Remove heavily infected leaves where possible.
  • Use recommended fungicides only if infection crosses threshold.
Market Signal

Price watch & planning

Experts suggest tracking nearby mandi prices for your crop over 7–10 days instead of selling on the first rise. Sudden spikes are often followed by correction if arrivals increase.

  • Compare at least 2–3 nearby markets.
  • Check arrivals trend along with price.
  • Plan staggered selling instead of single-day bulk.
Risk & Safety

Safe spray window guidance

Agronomists recommend avoiding pesticide sprays during high wind speed or strong afternoon heat to reduce drift and residue risk.

  • Prefer calm hours: early morning or after 4 PM.
  • Always use PPE: mask, gloves, covered clothing.
  • Follow label dose and pre-harvest interval strictly.
